### 3\. Network, Hardware Automations & Integrations {#3.-network,-hardware-automations-&-integrations}

At the intersection of physical infrastructure and digital intelligence, Codevertex Africa Limited engineers seamless hardware-software synergies. This pillar focuses on eliminating manual operational bottlenecks through intelligent, real-time connectivity between field equipment and the cloud.

* **Integrated Weighing Solution (TruLoad):** Our flagship hardware-integrated solution is an all-around weighing platform for **Axle Load Management**. By linking high-precision IoT devices/sensors and cameras directly to our analytics core, TruLoad provides Government axle enforcement sectors, Commercial Weighbridge Owners, and logistics firms with tamper-proof data, automated compliance reporting, and real-time overload prevention and axle-load compliance across Africa.  
* **ISP & Network Automation:** Inspired by robust network automation frameworks, our solutions serve as a **"Zero-Touch"** command center for network infrastructure. We automate the entire network lifecycle \- from **Router provisioning** and captive portal authentication to automated service suspension and payment reconciliation—ensuring your network scales without increasing manual overhead.
